Vali incorporates advanced encryption techniques and robust firewalls to fortify its defenses. These measures protect sensitive data and create barriers to deter and mitigate unauthorized access attempts.

Third Layer: Stealth

The third layer focuses on stealthy behavior, making it arduous for attackers to detect and bypass Vali's defenses. By operating stealthily, Vali effectively hides its presence, thereby increasing the complexity and challenges for potential attackers.

Fourth Layer: Code Obfuscation

To hinder hackers' attempts to analyze or tamper with Vali's code, the fourth layer involves obfuscation. Code obfuscation obscures the server's code, making it difficult to understand and manipulate, further enhancing the security of the system.

Fifth Layer: AI-Based Intelligence

Vali's fifth layer incorporates an AI-based intelligence tool that constantly learns from past attacks. By comprehending hacker tricks and tactics, Vali adapts its defenses accordingly, staying one step ahead of potential threats. This layer ensures that Vali remains proactive and continuously evolves to counter emerging attack vectors.

Vali Prepares Defense System

Based on the gathered information and intelligence from previous attacks, Vali meticulously prepares its defense system. By leveraging its AI-driven insights, Vali optimizes its security measures to counter specific threats and vulnerabilities.

Sixth Layer: Code Injection

In the sixth layer, Vali executes its code on the attacker's machine, leveraging seemingly harmless images to inject its defensive mechanisms. This method allows Vali to gain control over the attacker's system, redirecting their resources towards stopping attacks on the main server.

Seventh Layer: Utilizing Rootkits and Covert Techniques

Operating silently and covertly, Vali employs rootkits and similar techniques in the seventh layer. By utilizing these resources on the attacker's machine, Vali effectively neutralizes their capacity to harm the main server, providing an additional layer of defense.
